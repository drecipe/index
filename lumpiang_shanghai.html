Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground pork (or chicken)
  • 1 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1 cup finely chopped green beans
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 egg (optional, for binding)
  • Lumpia wrappers (spring roll wrappers)
  • Water (to seal the wrappers)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground pork, carrots, green beans, onions, garlic, soy sauce, black pepper, and egg. Mix well until all ingredients are incorporated.
  2. Take a lumpia wrapper and place it on a clean, flat surface, with one corner facing you.
  3. Spoon about 1-2 tablespoons of the filling near the corner closest to you.
  4. Fold the corner over the filling, then fold the sides inward and roll it tightly. Seal the edge with a little water.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  6. Once hot, carefully add the wrapped lumpia in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  7. Fry until golden brown and crispy, about 3-5 minutes. Remove and drain on paper towels.
