Pancit Canton

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pancit canton noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1 cup bell pepper, sliced (any color)
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shrimp, or pork (optional)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish (optional)
  • Lemon or calamansi for serving

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a pot and cook the pancit canton noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sliced onion, and saut√© until fragrant and the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the carrots and bell pepper. Stir-fry for about 3-5 minutes until they are slightly tender. Then add the cabbage and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. If using cooked chicken, shrimp, or pork, add it to the pan and mix well.
  5. Add the cooked pancit canton noodles to the pan. Pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using). Toss everything together, ensuring the noodles are well coated with the sauce.
  6. Taste and add salt and pepper as needed. Stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  7. Garnish with sliced green onions and serve with lemon or calamansi on the side.
