Tinolang Manok

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 2-3 tablespoons fish sauce (optional)
  • 2 cups green papaya or chayote, sliced
  • 2-3 cups spinach or malunggay (moringa leaves)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1-2 tablespoons oil (for sautéing)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Sauté the garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Skim off any scum that rises to the surface.
  4. Add fish sauce (if using) and simmer for about 30 minutes, or until the chicken is tender.
  5. Add the green papaya or chayote and cook for another 5-10 minutes until tender.
  6. Stir in the spinach or malunggay and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
