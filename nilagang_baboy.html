Nilagang Baboy

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork (preferably belly or shank), cut into chunks
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2-3 potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 cup corn on the cob, cut into pieces
  • 1-2 stalks of celery, chopped
  • 2-3 cups cabbage, quartered
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fish sauce (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (like parsley or green onions) for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the pork and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Skim off any scum that rises to the surface to keep the broth clear.
  3. Add the quartered onion and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes until the pork is tender.
  4. Once the pork is tender, add the potatoes, corn, and celery. Cook until the potatoes are tender, about 10-15 minutes.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and fish sauce to taste.
  6. Finally, add the cabbage and cook for another 5-7 minutes until wilted.
  7. Remove from heat, garnish with fresh herbs, and serve hot with rice.
