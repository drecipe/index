Chopsuey

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken, beef, or tofu (sliced thin)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1 cup carrots (sliced)
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 cup snow peas (or your choice of veggies)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (for thickening)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 inch ginger (minced)
  • 2-3 tbsp vegetable or sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Slice your protein and vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the protein and stir-fry until cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add more oil if needed.
  5. Add garlic and ginger, stir-frying for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  6. Add the harder vegetables (like carrots and broccoli) first. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
  7. Add bell peppers, snow peas, and bean sprouts. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  8. Return the cooked protein to the skillet.
  9. Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Mix well.
  10. Add the cornstarch slurry to thicken the sauce. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens.
