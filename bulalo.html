Bulalo

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef shank (with bone)
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1-2 whole peppercorns
  • 2-3 pieces of corn, cut into chunks
  • 2-3 medium potatoes, quartered
  • 1 cup bok choy or leafy greens
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: fish sauce for added flavor

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the beef shank under cold water. In a large pot, add the beef and cover it with water. Bring to a boil and let it simmer for about 10 minutes. This helps remove impurities.
  2. Drain the water and rinse the beef again. In the same pot, add 8 cups of fresh water, the beef, onion, garlic, and peppercorns. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat to a simmer.
  3. Let it simmer for about 2 to 3 hours or until the beef is tender. You can skim off any foam that forms on the surface.
  4. Once the beef is tender, add the corn and potatoes. Cook for about 15-20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the bok choy or leafy greens and season with salt and pepper. You can also add fish sauce if desired. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
