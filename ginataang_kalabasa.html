Ginataang Kalabasa at Sitaw

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium kalabasa (squash), peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup sitaw (string beans), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Optional: cooked shrimp or meat (like pork)

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cube the kalabasa.
  2. Cut the sitaw into 2-inch pieces.
  3. Chop the onion, mince the garlic, and slice the ginger.
  4. In a pot, heat the cooking oil over medium heat.
  5. Add the onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onions are translucent.
  6. Add the cubed kalabasa and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Add the coconut milk and fish sauce (if using). Bring to a gentle boil.
  8. Lower the heat and let it simmer until the kalabasa is tender (about 10-15 minutes).
  9. Stir in the sitaw and cook for another 5-7 minutes until the beans are tender but still crisp.
  10. Taste and adjust with salt and pepper as needed. If you added meat or shrimp, make sure they’re heated through.
