Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch piece ginger (sliced)
  • 2-3 cups coconut milk
  • 2-3 medium potatoes (cubed)
  • 2-3 carrots (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 2-3 tablespoons curry powder (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 2-3 green chili peppers (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until fragrant and onions are translucent.
  3. Add the chicken pieces. Cook until they are browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in the curry powder and cook for a couple of minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add the coconut milk, fish sauce, bay leaf, and bring to a gentle simmer.
  6. Add the potatoes, carrots, and bell pepper. Stir well.
  7. Let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. Stir occasionally.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, and additional fish sauce if needed. Add green chili peppers for some heat, if desired.
